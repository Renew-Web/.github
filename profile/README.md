# Reclaiming the open web: Make web greater again

The web is changing. Search results are flooded with AI-generated noise. Original voices are getting harder to find. And as large language models scrape and regurgitate the work of creators, more of them are choosing to go dark — pulling their content behind walls, locking it away, or simply stopping.

We think that's a loss worth fighting.

**renew-web** is a collective effort to surface the real, human-made corners of the internet. We create lightweight web showcases — clean, searchable, discoverable — for creators who care about openness but don't want to spend their days wrestling with SEO.

## What we do

We build web frontends that make creators' work easier to find. That's it. Every repository here is a presentation layer; **all original content and intellectual property belongs to its respective creator.** We are stewards, not owners.

Now, we offer those pagse return back to internet:

- https://renew-web.github.io/anitama-backup/
- https://renew-web.github.io/Learn-Vim_zh_cn/


## Our commitments

- **Non-commercial.** This project generates no revenue and never will. We exist purely in service of discoverability.
- **No AI training. No distillation.** Content surfaced through this project may not be used to train, fine-tune, or distill any machine learning model — in whole or in part, directly or indirectly.
- **Creator-first.** If a creator asks us to remove their work, we do it. No questions, no delays.

## Join us

If you've felt the web getting smaller — if you've watched great work disappear into obscurity while algorithmic slop rises to the top — you already know why this matters.

We're looking for developers, designers, and writers who want to push back. Contributing is simple: bring a creator (or be one), help us build a showcase, and keep the open web alive.

Open an issue, start a discussion, or reach out directly. The door is open.
